Caro de Valk (b. 1997)

Based in Amsterdam.



Exploring the world through a fieldrecorder, sound, textile and sculptural work, I search for different encounters with these mediums and the narrative I create around / with them, controlled or completely out of hand - letting the material have space for growth.


By presenting elements of my work in an installation format, my aim is to create interventions and destabilizing codes of communication in the space, to let the viewer be the catalyst for encounter.
