Video installation based on storyline and drawings; 8:04 min, silver molded rings, glazed ceramic, deformed metal, epoxy, weaving 30 cm x 20 cm, wool hanging net, felt, woodcut, etching, found stones, oyster shells; metal structures 35 cm x 35 cm, tufted oyster 1,25 m x 80 cm.







Every touch draws you closer into this landscape, creating a state of interaction
which raises curiosity and a bit of fantasy, possible scenarios filled by a backstory.
Warmth of the fluffy oyster on the floor provokes a sensation. Cold metal surfaces build
distance whilst keeping their balance on a small platform.




Where is comforting technology?
Sometimes my eyes melt in blue light
It echoes in visual, but dissolves after seconds.




I materialized an artificial space based on a fiction story, to wrap the secret not so secrets in.
Soundscapes from underneath form a new layer. Very near, almost touching you to show
the sound.
